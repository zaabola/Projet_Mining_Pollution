How It Works

This module uses a U-Net segmentation model trained on satellite imagery to classify land use:

  1. Upload a satellite image of an area of interest
  2. The AI performs pixel-level segmentation to classify each area
  3. Results show a color-coded mask and an overlay on the original image
  4. Area statistics (percentage and pixel counts) are calculated for each zone
Red — Mining Site
Blue — Residential Area
Tip: Higher resolution satellite images produce more accurate segmentation results.
